About

The Craft of Production Sound

With over a decade in production sound and 43+ credits spanning feature films, television series, and documentary, Damon Sheridan brings adaptability and technical precision to every set.

His work ranges from major studio productions like Anaconda for Sony Pictures to the world's most remote recording environments — including Nauru, where he served as sound recordist on the island nation's first-ever film production. Whether operating boom on a multi-camera television series or managing location sound in extreme conditions, the priority is always clean, usable audio that serves the story.

Damon's background spans location sound recording, boom operation, sound editing, sound design, and broadcast operations with the ABC. He brings both field experience and post-production understanding to every project.

Services

What Waterforge Offers

Production Sound Recording

On-location sound recording for feature film, television, and documentary. Boom operation, wireless management, and multi-track mixing for productions of any scale.

Sound Editing & Design

Post-production sound editing, design, and mixing for short film, documentary, and independent productions. From dialogue cleanup to full sound design.

Location Audio Consulting

Pre-production consultation for challenging recording environments. Equipment specification, logistics planning, and technical problem-solving for remote or complex locations.

Photography & Media

Production stills, behind-the-scenes documentation, and supplementary media capture for productions and commercial projects.
